Our Programs

Individual & Family Recovery

Our peer counselors who are in recovery themselves couple their experience with professional education to help you or your loved one identify issues pertaining to substance abuse, and work to identify and solve common problems.

Rise Inspire Academy

We combine innovative learning methods with a strong peer culture in which all students identify as being active in their recovery and committed to supporting their fellow students as they face the challenges associates with early recovery.

Youth Recovery Outreach

We show that it is possible to have fun without getting high. Participation is important as they afford you or your loved one the opportunity to relax and laugh, which is crucial to healing individually and as a family.
